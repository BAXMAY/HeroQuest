# **App Name**: Deeds & Cheers

## Core Features:

- Volunteer Work Submission: Children can submit descriptions and photos of their volunteer work for approval.
- Points and Scoring: Allocate points for completed tasks and allow users to accumulate scores.
- Real-time Leaderboard: Display a real-time leaderboard showing the top volunteers.
- Submission Approval: An approver module to verify submissions before points are awarded.
- Community Gallery: AI suggests relevant volunteer opportunities to individual kids or groups based on trending submissions of deeds.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant green (#90EE90) for growth and positivity.
- Background color: Pale green (#E0F8E0), almost white, but of the same hue as the primary color, lending a calming influence.
- Accent color: Soft yellow (#FFEC8B) to add cheerful accents and highlight important elements.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif font, to combine modernity with a touch of warmth for a friendly and inviting feel.
- Use playful icons that symbolize various volunteer activities, like cleaning up trash (recycle icon), helping animals (paw print), and community service (people holding hands).
- Add subtle animations to reward users, such as points animating upward upon approval or celebratory icons when reaching new milestones.
